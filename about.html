About Us

RxPx is an award-winning, AI-supported, global digital SaaS platform designed specifically for the needs of life sciences. We make life easier for patients, caregivers and HCPs and help our customers deliver unparalleled value while differentiating their therapeutic brands.

Founded by Doctors and Patients

RxPx was co-founded by a passionate group of physicians and leaders. Our physicians saw the untapped potential of technology to help safely onboard and monitor patients on complex specialty medicines. Our CEO encountered gaps in evidence-based information, support and adherence tools with her own patient experience. Together, they formed RxPx and a unique platform of digital health solutions that are positively transforming the lives of patients around the globe. Patients form the heart of our product design process and we believe this is how we create more effective and personalized solutions.
